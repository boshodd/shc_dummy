The History of Sacred Heart Catholic Church

Initiated as a small community in 1980, Sacred Heart Church has now grown to have over 270 families who zealously participated in strengthening the faith handed over by St. Thomas, the apostle. From the time of its inception to the current day, with the help of Vicars and parishioners, Sacred Heart Church has strived to explore avenues to reach out spiritually and contribute to the social development of the community.

Timeline of the events

  • 1980 Inception of Bhayandar Syro-Malabar Community
  • 1985: Commencement of Malayalam Qurbana services every second Sunday
  • 1984: First Malayalam Qurbana celebrated by Rev. Fr. Chelsius C.M.I at Our Lady Of Nazareth Church.
  • 1988: Rev. Fr. Varghese Nadackal appointed as the first vicar of the newly formed Sacred Heart Church.
  • 1990: Efforts to build a prayer hall began under the leadership of the then Vicar Rev. Fr. Jose Ainickal.
  • 1992: His Excellency Mar Paul Chittilapilly laid the foundation stone for the Bhayander Sacred Heart Catholic Welfare Centre.
  • 1997: Formation of a 64 member committee to look after the construction of the Church
  • 1999: Construction of the Church commences
  • 2001: Consecration of the newly constructed Sacred Heart Church and inaugration of Bhayandar Sacred Heart Welfare Centre.
  • 2009: Formation of the Sacred Heart Catholic Trust
  • 2010: Sacred Heart Church officially erected as a parish
  • 2011: Sacred Heart Church Bhayandar celebrates its 10th Anniversary of the construction of the Church.
  • 2013: Celebration of the Jubilee year marking the completion of 25 Years of existence in Bhayandar.
  • 2017: Renovation of the Altar
