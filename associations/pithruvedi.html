Pithruvedi



Pithruvedi Sacred Heart Bhayander unit consist of 165 members who are actively participating in activities organized in our church. Activities that were conducted by Pithruvedi were impacted due to lock down but Prithruvedi takes pride in mentioning that we were able to continue to provide the food kit to the deserving families. Pithruvedi members were also actively involved in checking the wellbeing of the bed ridden and old agged parishoners during this period. New Office bearers were elected in zoom meeting that was held dated 18th July, 2021.

Pithruvedi members actively participated in the Rosary that was organized for the releif from Pandamic in the month of October. We organised an online Bible quiz for the parishnors in collaberation with SHC Mathrusangham. Pithruveidi Members met on 19th December to make a year plan under the guidance of Rev. Fr Binoy and decided to celebrate Prithrivedi day on 19th March, 2022. Registration for NoRKA and Registration for Voter ID was part of the agenda in the meeting. Members disctributed the registration form for NOrka to Parishnors and working on the registeration of the Voter ID. Justin John decided to resign from the post of Vice president due to his commitment as the trustee of Sacred heart church and time contstraints, Mr. Manoj Samuel took incharge as the new Vice President of Pithruvedi.Since 2022 the budget for Food Kit distribution is raised to Rs 6500.

We celebrated our Pithruvedi day on 19th March with Holy Mass. All the Fathers Above 60 years of age were felicitated with a gift. All Pithruvedi members offered flower as offettery during the Holy Mass. Following day 20th march , we had cultural programs where all the members actively participated in entertaining the viewers and also Pithrivedi Organised Food as Oot Nercha for all the parishnors. We strive to have many more active participation in events to be a vibrant part of the church in contributions towards our society.
