Mathrusangam



Under the strong and dynamic leadership of Rev. Fr. Binoy, Mother Superior Vimal Maria, President Mrs. Annie Varghese, Secretary Mrs. Saya Paul, Treasurer Mrs. Elizabeth Kuriachan and committee members, our mathrusangam is working very effectively. We have 180 members. Few main activities are listed below:

LITURGICAL

Mathrusangam members are leading rosary everyday before Holy Mass. Mathrusangam with the support of prayer group initiated an online grocery for 24 hours x 31 days in the month of October and dedicated rosary everyday. Mathrusangam had conducted special prayers to fight against corona. They have recited around 9500 rosaries (- highest count from our Forane) for the intention of Covid affected people which was conducted by the International Syro Malabar Community. Mathrusangam had also actively participated in prayer marathon conducted by Diocese of Kalyan mathrusangam.

In the Month of December, all members recited ejaculatory prayers to welcome Infant Jesus in our hearts. To get enriched by Word of God we are uploading Bible - one chapter everyday in our WhatsApp group, with it's explanation which helps to understand more on Word of God. Conducted "Parihaara Yatra" for Nashik and visited 7 churches in this Lenten Season. Also took the responsibilities of liturgical assistances and church cleaning in special occasions.

SOCIAL ACTIVITIES & CHARITY:

In October Mathrusangam had collected groceries everyday from church members to help poor and the needy and received cash of Rs. 40,000. We have handed it over to Santwanagram, (orphanage) Kalyan. Mathrudinam had collected clothes from church members and gave to Kalyan mathrusangam to help poor and the needy. Mathrudinam was celebrated on 8th September with Solemn Mass followed by a seminar on 12th September. The subject was "mental stress of current situation and health issues of women". Faculty (online) : Sr. Dr. Jisha - was the guest speaker. After the session, online cultural program were there and all the members enjoyed it. Also to motivate mothers talents they had conducted Solo singing competition at Mathrudinam day. Women's day was celebrated and conducted an exhibition on Famous women Personalities, done by our different units of Parish. It gave insight / inspiration to all the members.

ENTERTAINMENT:

To relieve the daily pressure of mothers a movie was projected were all the mothers were able to relax and spend time with other mothers to which there was a great participation and they had fun. enjoyed well.

SKILL DEVELOPMENT:

To develop the skill of rosary making among the parish Bhayander Mathrusangam had hosted a Rosary making workshop. A lot of the church members participated and learnt to make rosary.
